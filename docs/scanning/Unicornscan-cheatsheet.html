Unicornscan cheatsheet

Unicornscan is a tool for information gathering and security audits.

Example scans

us -H -msf -Iv [address] -p 1-65535
us -H -mU -Iv [address] -p 1-65535

Flags

SYN : -mT
ACK scan : -mTsA
Fin scan : -mTsF
Null scan : -mTs
Xmas scan : -mTsFPU
Connect Scan : -msf -Iv
Full Xmas scan : -mTFSRPAU
scan ports 1 through 5 : (-mT) host:1-5
